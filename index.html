PeerStreet

The Startup Exchange Market

Raising Money as a startup is hard. It requires a notary, money and a tremendous effort. It is too complicated for the mayority of the people. But the popularity of crowdFunding and platforms like secondMarket show that more people are willing to invest into startups.

PeerStreet will allow people to do exactly that easily: raising money, investing in startups, back-finance your portfolio and much more.
